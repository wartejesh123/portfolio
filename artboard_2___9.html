BRIEF
Living in global world where there is exposure to entertainment content from all around the globe, there is a loss of cultural identity. Stories play a major role in inducing dreams to young adult and most of the escapist content are either American superheroes like Marvel and DC or folk tale reinterpretations by Disney.

From Ramayanam to Mahabaratha, Indian mythology is filled with enormously rich stories.
Mythology is the basis for value education in almost all cultures and the Mahabarata (especially the Bhagvadh Geeta) largely talks about Dharma, ie; self monitored ethical practices. And these stories , not only have answers to modern life ethical dilemmas but also deals with prevalent problems such as existential crisis and other societal, cultural problems such as dominant caste and such.

This is an issue cover design done in an attempt to retell the stories in a modern way
