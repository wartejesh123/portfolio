It is important for a designer to be exposed to the design language of famous designers. Here is a pastiche work of two of my favourite designers- Paul Rand and Paula Scher.
Paul Rand is the master of simplicity. He believes that graphic design has the ability to be a universal language, through geometry and forms. He changed the face of brands and cooperate identities .
Paula Scher is a designer who has managed to break many stereotypes in design and yet makes modish work. All her designs are exciting and visually powerful.
